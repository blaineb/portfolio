Concept testing early & often

We implemented a process of defining goals, writing scripts to evaluate concepts with users, and rigorously synthesizing the information afterwards.

Improving usability with simplicity & fun

They asked me to find simple, tactile ways to bring back paper sorting & categorization into the page.

We overhauled the messages section of the product to facilitate easier conversations and better triaging for those who received tons of messages.
