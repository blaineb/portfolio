The notepad for salespeople

User experience, research, visual design

Sync to Salesforce straight from your notes

Reps love connecting with clients. They hated ever touching Salesforce. We designed a notepad that let them update anything from their notes.

"We don't want to feel like B2B software"

Sales tools are all stuffy. We wanted to come across as approachable, & fun.

We picked a friendly color palette, colloquial typeface, and made the copy as personable as we could.

It made our app feel like a tool made for reps, not their managers.
